Not Sure Where to Start

A Quick Way to Choose

You own a bike or scooter

Start with Two Wheeler — third-party is legally mandatory, comprehensive protects the bike itself.

You own a car

Start with Four Wheeler — same mandatory-vs-optional structure, different rates and coverage details.

You use a vehicle for business income

Trucks, taxis, and fleet vehicles need Commercial Vehicle cover, not personal motor insurance — the rules and rates differ.

You're planning a trip abroad

Start with Travel Insurance — individual or family floater, Schengen visa requirements, and honest sum-insured guidance. See Travel Insurance →
